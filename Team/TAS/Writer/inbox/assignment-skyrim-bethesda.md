# Assignment: Upcoming Bethesda News Related to Skyrim

Write a news article covering the latest Bethesda announcements and developments related to The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim. This may include (but is not limited to):

- Anniversary editions, re-releases, or ports to new platforms
- Official statements from Bethesda or Todd Howard regarding Skyrim's legacy

- Headline must be accurate and specific -- no vague clickbait

- Target length: 400-600 words
- Include a brief intro paragraph summarizing the key news, followed by supporting detail
